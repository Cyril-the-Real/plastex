3 Enumerations and tables

  • \((\Phi \circ \Psi )_*X=\Phi _*\Psi _*X\)

  • \(\Phi ^*(\mathcal{L}_X\alpha )=\mathcal{L}_{\Phi ^{-1}_*X}\Phi ^*\alpha \)

  • \(\Phi ^*(i_X\alpha )=i_{\Phi ^{-1}_*X}\Phi ^*\alpha \)
